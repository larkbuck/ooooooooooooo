moon phases = global experience

2021

  • new + full moon posts for one lunar year == twelve lunar months == ~354 days
  • time-based: experimental, evolving, cyclical

sound 🔊 and symbol ✔️

  • light use of text = more accessible to speakers of all languages

intentions

  • leverage networked digital space to connect with the physical, natural world
  • build experimental reference of the moon's impact on planet + life-forms (beyond 💛astrology💛)
  • research past tradition + create new (virtual) rituals
  • honor ancestors persecuted for relationship to the moon: e.g. women + other targeted genders; people who practiced indigenous and earth-based healing practices
  • redefine feminine beyond biology (feminine moon ≠ menstrual cycles)
  • confront moon colonialism + tourism: NASA's Artemis Program, Artemis Project (private venture) , #dearMoon Project
  • develop nonverbal lexicons to communicate phases of the moon
